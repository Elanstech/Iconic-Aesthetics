Permanent Makeup FAQ

What is permanent makeup?

Permanent makeup (also known as cosmetic tattooing or micropigmentation) is a technique where pigments are implanted into the skin to create the appearance of makeup. It can be used for eyebrows, eyeliner, lips, and more, enhancing your natural features.

How long does permanent makeup last?

The results of permanent makeup typically last 1-3 years, depending on factors like skin type, lifestyle, and how well you take care of the treated area. Touch-ups are recommended to maintain the desired look over time.

Does it hurt?

The discomfort level varies by person, but most clients report minimal pain during the procedure. Numbing creams are applied to ensure you remain comfortable throughout the process.
