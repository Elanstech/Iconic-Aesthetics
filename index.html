Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do permanent makeup results last?

Permanent makeup typically lasts between 1-3 years, depending on your skin type, lifestyle, and aftercare. Touch-up appointments are recommended every 12-18 months to maintain the color and shape.

Is permanent makeup painful?

We apply a topical numbing cream before and during the procedure to minimize discomfort. Most clients describe the sensation as mild to moderate, similar to a scratching feeling rather than sharp pain.

How long do eyelash extensions last?

Eyelash extensions typically last for the natural growth cycle of your lashes, around 6-8 weeks. However, we recommend maintenance appointments every 2-3 weeks to fill in any lashes that have naturally shed.

How often should I get a facial?

For optimal results, we recommend getting a facial every 4-6 weeks, which aligns with your skin's natural cell turnover cycle. However, the frequency can vary based on your specific skin concerns and goals.

What should I do before my appointment?

To prepare for your appointment, avoid caffeine and alcohol 24 hours before, come with a clean face (no makeup), and avoid sun exposure or tanning for at least a week prior. Specific instructions will be provided based on your service.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! Gift cards are available for purchase in our spa or online. They make perfect gifts for birthdays, holidays, or any special occasion. Gift cards can be used for any service or product we offer.
