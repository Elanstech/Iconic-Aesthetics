Refund & Cancellation Policy

Please read our policies carefully before booking your appointment

Service Finality

All services rendered at Iconic Aesthetics are considered final. We take great care to ensure your satisfaction, and all treatments are performed by certified professionals using premium products.

  • All services are final — no refunds will be issued
  • Consultations are provided before all permanent makeup services
  • Aftercare instructions are given to ensure optimal results

Service Credits

While we don't offer refunds, we value your loyalty and satisfaction. Service credits may be applied toward future appointments.

  • Credits may be used toward other services
  • Credits are valid for 12 months from issue date
  • Credits are non-transferable and non-refundable
  • Touch-up sessions are subject to separate pricing

Cherry Package Policy

Our Cherry packages are specially priced bundles designed to give you the best value. These packages have specific terms:

  • Cherry packages are non-refundable once started
  • All services must be completed within the package timeframe
  • Unused services expire after 6 months
  • Package services cannot be transferred to another person

Cancellation & Rescheduling

We understand that schedules change. To maintain fairness to all clients and our team, we enforce the following policy:

  • 24-hour notice required to reschedule without penalty
  • Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice incur a fee
  • Emergency situations are handled on a case-by-case basis
  • Please call us directly to reschedule or cancel
