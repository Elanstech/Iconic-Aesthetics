About Me

Welcome! I’m Ilanit Sholomov, a New York City-based permanent makeup artist and aesthetician with a deep passion for helping people feel confident in their own skin. From a young age, I was drawn to the art of beauty. Friends and classmates would often compliment my makeup and tell me I had an artistic touch. That early love for enhancing beauty has grown into a fulfilling career where I now offer services like lash lifts, brow lamination, lip blushing, and nano blading.

I pride myself on providing personalized, one-on-one treatments that enhance your natural beauty. Every service is tailored to fit your unique style and features, whether you’re looking for a subtle enhancement or a bolder transformation. My goal is always to bring out the best version of you.

Your satisfaction and confidence are my top priorities. Whether it’s your first time or you’re a returning client, I’m here to ensure you leave my studio feeling radiant and refreshed. I’m excited to continue growing this business I love while building meaningful connections with each of you.

Thank you for trusting me to be a part of your beauty journey!
