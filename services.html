Our Services

Brow Microshading

A semi-permanent technique that uses small dots of pigment to create a soft, powdered look, perfect for achieving fuller, well-defined brows.

Nanoblading

Nanoblading is a precision eyebrow treatment that uses ultra-fine needles to create natural-looking, hair-like strokes for perfectly shaped brows.

Combo - Brows & Nano Blading

A combination of microshading and nanoblading to give you fuller, more defined brows that still look soft and natural.

Lip Blushing

A semi-permanent cosmetic tattoo that enhances the natural color of your lips, providing a soft blush effect for a more youthful look.

Lip Neutralization

A treatment that corrects dark or cool tones in the lips, leaving a more even, neutral base for enhanced color and vibrancy.

Semi Permanent Eyeliner

A cosmetic tattooing procedure that adds definition to the lash line, giving you the appearance of always wearing perfectly applied eyeliner.

Scar Camouflage

A paramedical tattooing technique used to conceal scars by blending them with the surrounding skin tone, providing a more uniform appearance.

Inkless Stretch Mark Rejuvenation

A technique that helps to reduce the appearance of stretch marks without using pigment, promoting smoother and healthier skin.

Eye Lash Extensions - Classic, 2D, 3D

A treatment that lifts and curls your natural lashes, followed by a tint to make them appear longer, fuller, and more defined.

Eye Lash Lifting & Tint

A treatment that lifts and curls your natural lashes, followed by a tint to make them appear longer, fuller, and more defined.

Brow Lamination w/ Tint

A brow styling treatment that straightens and sets brow hairs in place, followed by a tint for a fuller, defined look.

Combo Lash Lift & Brow Lamination w/ Tint

A combination treatment that includes a lash lift and brow lamination, both with tint, to enhance and define your eyes and brows.

30 Min Express Facial

A quick but effective facial treatment designed to refresh your skin, perfect for those on the go.

60 Min European Facial

A luxurious facial treatment that deeply cleanses, exfoliates, and nourishes the skin, leaving it feeling refreshed and rejuvenated.

Dermaplaning Facial

A facial treatment that exfoliates the skin using a surgical blade to remove dead skin cells and peach fuzz, resulting in a smoother complexion.
